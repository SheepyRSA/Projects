Who is PConnect?

We are a consultancy established in 2019 to assist organisations achieve exceptional results by finding and implementing practical solutions to complex issues from S.H.E.Q (Safety, Health, Environmental & Quality) Management Systems. We offer our clients hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly retainers to assist them with the management of their S.H.E.Q management systems.

Our clients make use of digital compliance systems to create powerful, sustainable change by disrupting existing thinking patterns. We also help you to create an environment where your employees feel engaged, by communicating and inspiring them.

We will provide you with the clarity to take action in order to help your company grow and your people to succeed.

In addition to extensive expertise, we have lived on both sides of the desk.
