Health and Safety Consulting

Porter and Connections provides a vast range of Health and Safety Consulting services to its clients.

Health and Safety Consulting Services by PConnect assist organizations to comply with the basic health and safety requirements as specified in the Occupational Health and Safety Act 85 of 1993 and the relevant regulations applicable to the organizations scope.

Companies that are only interested in complying with South African laws concerning

Occupational Health and Safety Act 85 of 1993 can use this option.

As a core service at Porter and Connections, we provide clients with full Health and Safety compliance and assist them with the requirements and specifications for Health and Safety.

Our services at Porter and Connections include the following inside of our retainer services options:

    • Placement of Health & Safety Professionals
    • Health & Safety Files
    • L.M.E. Registrations
    • A.I.A Registrations
    • Professional & Trade Member Registrations
    • In-house Health & Safety Certifications
    • Legal Compliance Audits
    • Legal Registers
